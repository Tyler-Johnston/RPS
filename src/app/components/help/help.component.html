The Basics

You will see an "Opponent's Move" such as "Rock", "Paper", or "Scissors" You need to counter this move with your own move.

Rock beats Scissors. Paper beats Rock. Scissors beats Paper.

Optionally, create an account to sync your progress across devices.

Strategy

Each win grants you the number of points equal to your PPW (Points-Per-Win). You can increase your PPW via Shop Upgrades and by maininting a higher streak.

The goal is to automate your wins, but you will need to generate resources to keep this going long-term.
